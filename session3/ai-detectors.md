# AI Detectors (optional exercise)

There are a number of online providers that allow PDFs to be examined for AI-generated text or plagiarism. Download [uni_leipzig.pdf](uni_leipzig.pdf) and test such a system. Although the text is obviously AI-generated, the AI detectors fail to recognize it as such. Why?
* [isgen.ai](https://isgen.ai)
* [hipdf.com](https://www.hipdf.com/)
* [scribbr.de/plagiatspruefung/](https://www.scribbr.de/plagiatspruefung/)
